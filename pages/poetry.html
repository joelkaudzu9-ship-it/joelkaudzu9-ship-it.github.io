THREADS OF BECOMING

a collection of verse & vulnerability
"These poems are not just words — they are the echoes of a soul learning to breathe again."

Joel George Kaudzu

Premed Dental Surgery Student · Poet · Innovator

Joel Kaudzu is a premed dental surgery student at Kamuzu University of Health Sciences, a poet, an innovator, and a lifelong learner. From an early age, Joel has been curious, disciplined, and reflective, constantly exploring the world around him and seeking to understand life on a deeper level.

His journey has not been without struggle. During secondary school, Joel experienced a period of intense self-consciousness and social anxiety. Friends misunderstood his situation, and this deeply affected him. Once a confident extrovert, Joel began withdrawing from social situations, avoiding crowds, and even hesitating to attend religious meetings. Yet, in this period of isolation, he discovered resilience, introspection, and a powerful desire for personal growth.

Despite these challenges, Joel never allowed adversity to define him. He invested in himself outside of formal schooling, devouring books about business, exploring the stock market, and learning new skills. He began building connections, seeking mentorship, and understanding people, family, and the place of God in life. His creativity and innovative mindset led him to experiment with technology, eventually developing his first app, CheckMySmile.

Through poetry, Joel channels his deepest emotions, reflections, and observations about life, society, and the human condition. His work explores pain, perseverance, hope, innovation, and the infinite thoughts that shape our experiences. Each poem reflects his personal journey, offering readers insight into both the challenges he has faced and the resilience he has cultivated.

Beyond academia and writing, Joel is passionate about connecting with others, learning from diverse experiences, and making a positive impact through innovation and creativity. Mentors and peers, such as Joshua, have played significant roles in shaping his spiritual and personal growth, guiding him toward deeper understanding and meaningful action.

Joel's story is one of perseverance, creativity, and self-discovery. From battling insecurities and social pressures to embracing technology, innovation, and poetry, he continues to learn, adapt, and create. His journey is a testament to the power of resilience, reflection, and faith, inspiring others to embrace their vulnerabilities, pursue their passions, and navigate life with courage, integrity, and vision.
