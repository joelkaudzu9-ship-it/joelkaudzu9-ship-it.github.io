My Leadership Principles

See Potential

Every person has more inside than they show. Leadership is seeing that and calling it forth.

Serve First

Before you can lead, you must serve. I clean the whiteboard, I stay late, I ask "how can I help?"

Take Responsibility

When things go wrong, I own it. When they go right, the team gets credit.

Grow Continuously

A leader who stops learning stops leading. I read, I study, I stay curious.
