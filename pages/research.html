Research & Innovation

Bridging dentistry, immunology, and data science

🔬 Primary Research Interests

  • Dental-Immunology Interactions

    Exploring how oral microbiome affects systemic immunity, and potential diagnostic markers in saliva for immune disorders.

  • Predictive Analytics in Oral Health

    Using machine learning to predict dental disease progression based on lifestyle, genetic, and environmental factors.

  • Low-Cost Diagnostic Tools

    Developing affordable, AI-assisted diagnostic devices for rural clinics with limited access to dentists.

  • Chromosomal Aberrations & Oral Health

    Investigating links between genetic abnormalities and dental development, with focus on early detection.
