Primary Research Interests

Exploring the intersections where innovation meets impact

Dental-Immunology Interactions

Exploring how oral microbiome affects systemic immunity, and potential diagnostic markers in saliva for immune disorders. Investigating correlations between periodontal health and autoimmune conditions.

Immunology Microbiome Dentistry

Predictive Analytics in Oral Health

Using machine learning to predict dental disease progression based on lifestyle, genetic, and environmental factors. Developing risk assessment tools for early intervention.

Machine Learning Data Science Predictive Models

Low-Cost Diagnostic Tools

Developing affordable, AI-assisted diagnostic devices for rural clinics with limited access to dentists. Focus on smartphone-based imaging and remote consultation platforms.

AI MedTech Rural Health
